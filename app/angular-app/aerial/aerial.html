Aerial Surveying

Licensed by the FAA

The FAA requires that all commercial operators of sUAV’s (Drones) be licensed and be proficient in Aeronautics and Air Space Regulations. Cinescape operators carry section 107 certifications and are fully insured giving our customers complete piece mind. Our staff are trained to work with the FAA and Air Traffic Control Towers in the event that operating limitations occur due to flight restrictions or proximity to public airports.

Well Equipped

Whether you’re looking for a simple real estate presentation or a full commercial production, we have a wide range of the latest cameras, sUAV’s and Post Production equipment to handle any size job. Our sUAV’s are equipped with fully autonomous flight automation systems allowing us to perform complex shots that are difficult to achieve while flying manually.
